Hire and Promotion

Builder

Builders are responsible for creating and designing various structures within the server. They must possess a combination of creative skills and technical knowledge.

Requirements:

  • General and basic knowledge of WorldEdit
  • Familiarity with WorldEdit addons like Vortex
  • Portfolio or images showcasing previous builds
  • Basic knowledge of the base game mechanics

Optional:

  • Have previous experience with creating dungeons or simular
  • Know color palets

Developer

Developers play a crucial role in implementing server features, fixing bugs, and optimizing performance. They must have strong programming skills and a deep understanding of Minecraft mechanics.

Requirements:

  • Basic knowledge of GUIs, functions, and their returns
  • Familiarity with mob plugins and mechanics
  • At least 6 months of Skript experience
  • Previous work on at least 1 server
  • Ability to work effectively in a team environment

Optional:

  • Experience with resource packs or pixel art
  • Experience with HTML, css, javascript
  • Experience with SQL databases

Helper

Helpers provide assistance to players, answer questions, and enforce server rules. They must have excellent communication skills and a thorough understanding of server policies.

Requirements:

  • Good understanding of server rules and policies
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to remain calm and patient in stressful situations
