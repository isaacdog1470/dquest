Map Layouts and Enemies

Sahara Desert (0-10)

The Sahara Desert is a vast expanse of sand and dunes, known for its scorching temperatures and harsh conditions. It is home to various enemies, including:

  • Husks
  • Skeletons
  • Rare Sand Elemental

Forest (10-20)

The forest is a dense and lush area with towering trees and abundant wildlife. Adventurers will encounter the following enemies:

  • Zombies
  • Skeletons
  • Forest Spirit

Heroic Cliffs (20-30)

The Heroic Cliffs are towering cliffs overlooking vast landscapes. Brave souls who venture here will face formidable enemies such as:

  • Undeads
  • Goblins
  • Ancient Hero
